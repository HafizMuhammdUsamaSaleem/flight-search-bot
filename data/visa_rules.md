# Visa & Entry Rules for UAE Nationals
(UAE passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry date unless stated otherwise)

### Japan (Tokyo)
- Visa-free entry for up to **90 days** for tourism or business.
- No registration required.
- Return/onward ticket recommended.

### United States (New York)
- **ESTA required** (Electronic System for Travel Authorization).
- Apply online at least 72 hours before departure — cost $21, valid 2 years.
- Visa-free stay up to **90 days**.
- No onward ticket needed if entering by air.

### United Kingdom (London)
- **ETA required** from **2025** (Electronic Travel Authorisation) — £10, valid 2 years.
- Visa-free stay up to **6 months**.
- Biometric UAE passport holders can use e-gates on arrival.

### France (Paris) — Schengen Area
- Visa-free entry for up to **90 days in any 180-day period**.
- Covers all Schengen countries.
- Must carry proof of accommodation & sufficient funds (€120/day if no hotel booking).

### Australia (Sydney)
- **eVisitor visa (subclass 651)** — free, apply online, valid 12 months.
- Multiple entries, each stay up to **3 months**.
- Must be outside Australia when applying; processing < 24 hrs.

# Airline Refund & Change Policies
(Applies to tickets booked directly with the airline)

### Emirates
- **Refundable tickets**: Full refund minus AED 200 fee if cancelled 48+ hrs before departure.
- **Non-refundable**: Change allowed for AED 400 fee + fare difference; no cash refund.

### Qatar Airways
- **Refundable**: 100% refund (minus 10% processing fee) up to 24 hrs before departure.
- **Non-refundable**: Free date change within 30 days; AED 300 fee beyond that.

### Turkish Airlines
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus €50 fee up to 12 hrs before flight.
- **Non-refundable**: Rebooking allowed for €75 + fare difference.

### Lufthansa
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus €100 fee up to 24 hrs before departure.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee €150 + fare difference; no refund.

### All Nippon Airways (ANA)
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus ¥5,000 fee up to 24 hrs before departure.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee ¥10,000 + fare difference.

### Japan Airlines (JAL)
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus ¥5,400 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee ¥10,800 + fare difference.

### United Airlines
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus $200 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: 24-hr free cancellation; after that $200 change fee.

### Delta Air Lines
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus $199 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Free change on most fares; $200 fee for Basic Economy.

### British Airways
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus £50 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee £100 + fare difference.

### Virgin Atlantic
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus £75 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee £150 + fare difference.

### Air France / KLM
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus €100 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee €150 + fare difference.

### Qantas
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus AUD 99 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee AUD 199 + fare difference.

### Singapore Airlines
- **Refundable**: Full refund minus SGD 100 fee.
- **Non-refundable**: Change fee SGD 150 + fare difference.

# General Tips
- Always check airline website for latest policy (subject to change).
- Travel insurance recommended for medical or trip cancellation coverage.
- UAE residents: Ensure residency visa has 6+ months validity when returning.
